# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: Is WomenSkillHub free?**
A: Yes! The platform is completely free to use. All features are available without any paid subscription.

**Q: Do I need to pay for courses?**
A: No. All courses are free. Instructors can optionally set prices, but most are free.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes! You can download course modules for offline access. Progress syncs when you go online.

## Getting Started

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Click "Sign Up" on the homepage, fill in your details, and choose your role (Student, Instructor, Seller, etc.).

**Q: What roles are available?**
A: Student (learner), Instructor (course creator), Seller (marketplace), Mentor, Field Agent, and Admin.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. (Feature coming soon)

## Courses

**Q: How do I enroll in a course?**
A: Go to Courses, find a course you like, and click "Enroll Now".

**Q: Can I get a certificate?**
A: Yes! Complete all lessons and quizzes to earn a certificate.

**Q: Can I download course materials?**
A: Yes! You can download lessons as PDFs and videos for offline viewing.

## Marketplace

**Q: How do I sell products?**
A: Sign up as a Seller, go to Marketplace, and click "List Product".

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Payments are processed monthly. Funds are transferred to your registered bank account.

**Q: What's the commission?**
A: Currently 0% commission during beta. This may change in the future.

## Financial Tools

**Q: Is my financial data secure?**
A: Yes! All financial data is encrypted and stored securely.

**Q: Can I export my budget data?**
A: Yes! You can export as CSV or PDF.

**Q: How accurate is the EMI calculator?**
A: The calculator uses standard EMI formulas. Actual EMI may vary based on bank policies.

## Cyber Safety

**Q: Is the phishing simulation real?**
A: No, it's a safe simulation for learning purposes only.

**Q: Can I report real incidents?**
A: Yes! Use the "Report Incident" feature. You can report anonymously.

**Q: Where do reports go?**
A: Reports are logged in the system and can be shared with authorities if needed.

## Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Is the app mobile-friendly?**
A: Yes! The app is fully responsive and works on mobile devices.

**Q: Can I use the app on multiple devices?**
A: Yes! Your account syncs across all devices.

**Q: What's the minimum internet speed needed?**
A: 1 Mbps is recommended. Offline mode works without internet.

## Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email support@womenskillhub.com or use the in-app chat.

**Q: How long does support take to respond?**
A: Usually within 24 hours.

**Q: Is there a community forum?**
A: Yes! Join our community groups for peer support.

## Data & Privacy

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.

**Q: What happens to my data when I delete my account?**
A: All personal data is deleted within 30 days. Course progress is archived.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings > Data Export to download your data.

**Q: Is my data shared with third parties?**
A: No. We never share personal data without your consent.

## Troubleshooting

**Q: The app won't load**
A: Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.

**Q: I can't log in**
A: Check your email and password. Make sure Caps Lock is off.

**Q: Videos won't play**
A: Check your internet connection. Try a different browser.

**Q: I'm not receiving emails**
A: Check your spam folder. Add support@womenskillhub.com to your contacts.

Can't find your answer? [Contact us](mailto:support@womenskillhub.com) or check our [documentation](docs/).
